<text>
<idea>
  <design_movement>Neo-Brutalism / Pop Art</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    1. **Bold & Playful:** High contrast, thick borders, and vibrant colors to make AI feel less intimidating and more fun.
    2. **Direct & Honest:** Clear typography, no hidden menus, everything is upfront.
    3. **Gamified Interaction:** Big buttons, exaggerated hover states, and confetti-like feedback.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    Uses a high-contrast palette to evoke energy and excitement.
    - **Background:** Off-white (#F0F0F0) or soft yellow (#FEFCE8)
    - **Primary:** Electric Blue (#2563EB) for actions
    - **Accent:** Hot Pink (#DB2777) for highlights/correct answers
    - **Text:** Stark Black (#000000) for maximum readability
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **Card-Based Stack:** Questions appear on "cards" that slide in or stack. The layout is slightly asymmetrical with offset shadows (hard shadows, no blur).
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    - **Hard Shadows:** `box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px 0px #000;`
    - **Thick Borders:** 2px-3px solid black borders on all interactive elements.
    - **Retro Icons:** Pixel art or simple line icons for AI concepts.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **Tactile Feedback:** Buttons should feel like physical switches. Clicking a button shifts it down to mimic a press.
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    **Snappy & Bouncy:** Transitions are fast (spring physics). No slow fades; elements pop in.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - **Headings:** 'Space Mono' or 'Chivo Mono' (Monospace) for a techy but retro feel.
    - **Body:** 'Inter' or 'Public Sans' for readability, but with heavy weights (SemiBold).
  </typography_system>
</idea>

<idea>
  <design_movement>Soft Glassmorphism / Ethereal Tech</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    1. **Approachable & Calm:** AI can be overwhelming; this design soothes the user with soft gradients and blurs.
    2. **Transparency & Depth:** Using frosted glass effects to layer content, suggesting depth of knowledge.
    3. **Fluidity:** Everything flows gently, mimicking the adaptive nature of AI.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    Pastel gradients representing the "cloud" and intelligence.
    - **Background:** Mesh gradient of Soft Violet (#E0E7FF), Pale Cyan (#CFFAFE), and White.
    - **Glass:** White with 60% opacity and backdrop blur.
    - **Text:** Deep Slate (#1E293B) for contrast without harshness.
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **Floating Container:** The quiz lives in a central, glass-like container floating in a colorful, moving void.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    - **Frosted Glass:** `backdrop-filter: blur(12px); background: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.6);`
    - **Soft Glows:** Elements have diffuse colored shadows instead of black shadows.
    - **Rounded Corners:** Large border-radius (24px+) for a friendly feel.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **Gentle Guidance:** Hover states are subtle glows or slow lifts. Transitions are smooth dissolves.
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    **Float & Flow:** Background gradients slowly morph. Questions fade in/out gracefully.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - **Headings:** 'Outfit' or 'Plus Jakarta Sans' (Geometric Sans) for a modern, friendly look.
    - **Body:** 'Quicksand' or 'Nunito' for rounded, approachable text.
  </typography_system>
</idea>

<idea>
  <design_movement>Swiss Style / International Typographic Style</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    1. **Clarity & Order:** Grid-based, objective, and highly readable. Perfect for educational content.
    2. **Typography First:** Large, bold type does the heavy lifting. Images are secondary.
    3. **Asymmetric Balance:** Dynamic layouts that aren't just centered text.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    Minimalist with one strong signal color.
    - **Background:** White (#FFFFFF) or very light gray (#F8FAFC).
    - **Primary:** International Orange (#FF4500) or Swiss Red (#D80000).
    - **Text:** Black (#000000).
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **Split Screen / Grid:** On desktop, question on left, options on right (or vice versa). Strong alignment lines.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    - **Big Type:** Questions are treated as headlines.
    - **Grid Lines:** Subtle or explicit dividers between sections.
    - **Geometric Shapes:** Simple circles or squares to denote progress.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **Instant & Precise:** No bounce, no blur. Hover states invert colors (black bg, white text).
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    **Slide & Reveal:** Content slides in from the side or bottom along grid lines.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - **Headings:** 'Helvetica Now' (or 'Inter' Tight) - Bold, tight tracking.
    - **Body:** 'Roboto' or 'Inter' - Neutral, legible.
  </typography_system>
</idea>
</text>
